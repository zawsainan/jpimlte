About Us

Mr. Brandon Lye

Brandon has over 25 years of corporate, consulting and investment experience across Asia, specializing in investment, merger and acquisition and strategic planning. Brandon's professional experience includes a role as a Director at PricewaterhouseCoopers Singapore (PwC Singapore), where he led the Deals advisory practice. Brandon has worked with both public and private sectors including regional governments, MNCs and local SMEs and conglomerates. Brandon has gained deep experiences in deals structuring and investment in the real estate, infrastructure, financial services and technology sectors.

Before joining PwC, Brandon served as the Executive Vice President at Surbana Jurong, a Singapore's Temasek owned global urban, infrastructure and managed services consulting firm. In this capacity, he led the firm's M&A strategy by spearheading successful M&A transactions in China, Singapore and Australia. Brandon also held the role of CEO at an asset management company associated with a European family office operation, where he oversaw successful equity and debt financing with international financial institutions including Asia Development Bank (ADB).

Brandon holds a Executive Master of Business Administration (EMBA) from Nanyang Business School and a Bachelor of Engineering (Hons) from Nanyang Technological University. He also attended numerous executive programs including Advanced Management Program at Hass School of Business, University of California Berkley, Senior Executive Management Program at Tsinghua University, China and the Strategic Management Program, NUS Business School, Singapore.

Mr. Jonathan Poh

Currently a Senior Partner at a reputable External Asset Management firm in Singapore, Jonathan brings with him over 25 years of invaluable banking and finance experience as the Executive Director with Deutsche Bank and UBS AG. Prior to his positions in Deutsche Bank and UBS AG, Jonathan's experiences also include senior Client Advisor roles in Citibank and American Express Private Banking.

In his years as a personal and corporate financial and wealth advisor, Jonathan has formulated plans and solutions for numerous Ultra-High Net-Worth clients in the Southeast Asia region. His commitment and dedication to his clients have helped him achieve several Service Excellence Awards throughout his career.

Jonathon's knowledge of financial asset management is demonstrated in his exceptional expertise and proficiency in various aspects of the financial industry, both locally and internationally.
